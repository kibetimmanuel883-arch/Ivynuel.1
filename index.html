NUEL
A letter to the one who made me believe in love

Dear Shiku,

This is the part of the story that never got spoken out loud. The part that stayed folded inside me long after silence became the loudest thing between us.

IVYNUEL exists because some things deserve to be said once — clearly, gently, and without interruption.

I haven't a single regret about being with you. Besides everything, it has been a journey of love, laughter, and memories that will forever remain in my heart. It has helped me to learn more about myself and the depth of what we shared. This love we shared taught me patience, tenderness and how fully my heart can open to someone I love.

And now, I am ready to move forward with a heart full of gratitude and a soul that remembers you with love.

You mattered. What we had mattered. And even though you chose absence, I chose to remember with grace. You will always have a place in my heart mi dama.

— Your Sweet Sir

Every shade of lilac, every pause, every breath of space was shaped by the light you once brought into my life.

London

For days on end, I always dreamt of us in our city in our corvette driving through the night, windows down, music blasting, and just being together. These little imaginations kept me going. And they still do.

Sydney

There were moments when time seemed to stand still, and in those fleeting seconds, I felt an overwhelming sense of connection with you. Those memories are etched in my heart forever.

Aurora Village, Ivalo

I used to imagine us together under the Northern Lights, wrapped in warmth and wonder. Those dreams of us exploring the world together kept my spirit alive.

Four Seasons, Seychelles

I often found myself daydreaming about us on a serene beach in Seychelles, the sun setting over the ocean as we walked hand in hand. Those visions of tranquility and togetherness were my escape from reality.

As we talked about all the places we were going to visit, I truly believed I would take you there one day. I wanted to make you as happy as you could possibly be—because that was always my goal. It was never just talk. I promised myself I would do everything I could to turn those dreams into reality. And even though you are gone now, that belief, that memory, will always drive me. Even though that means going on without you by my side.

Before We Let Everything Go,
Let's look back, Shall We?

As I embark on these journeys alone, I carry with me the echoes of our shared dreams. Each destination becomes a tribute to the love we once shared, a testament to the dreams we dared to dream together. And I carry the memories that I refuse to let go of, because they are a part of who I am now.

We can't forget the first video you sent me where our friend 'Onyi' was singing my favourite song. I always remember this and laugh at the good old days. Those memories are precious to me.

As you go, keep them close — Ben, PP, and Mr. Carrot.

I know this is your choice, and I respect it.

When I’m no longer beside you, let them sit with you in the quiet and remind you that you are safe.

They carry the care I gave freely, without condition.

Nothing we shared is being undone by this — some things were real, and they remain.

If you ever need grounding, peace, or a moment to breathe, hold Ben, PP, or Mr. Carrot close and remember that what I gave you was genuine.

This is me accepting, with grace, you going away, so you can leave with closure and without weight.

Every now and then, I found myself listening to the audio message you sent me. Your voice, filled with warmth and sincerity, brings back a flood of memories. It's a bittersweet reminder of the connection we once shared, and it comforts me in moments of solitude. You always had a beautiful singing voice, and hearing it again makes me feel closer to you, even in your absence.

One thing about you that always interested me was your taste in music. You had a way of finding songs that spoke to the soul, and sharing them with me felt like sharing a piece of yourself. This was the first song you ever sent me, and it will always hold a special place in my heart.

And finally, there is your beautiful smile and your gorgeous eyes—though even those words feel like understatements.

Your smile isn’t just an expression; it’s a sudden shift in the room’s atmosphere, a quiet dawn that breaks through whatever gravity the day has held. It carries a kind of effortless grace that makes everything around you feel a little more certain and a lot more bright.

As for your eyes, they are where I always find myself getting lost. They have this incredible depth, a mixture of clarity and mystery that seems to hold a thousand unspoken stories. When you look at me, it’s like being seen in the truest sense—caught in a gaze that is as kind as it is captivating.

Together, they are the harmony I never knew I was listening for, and honestly, the most beautiful view I’ve ever had the privilege to know.

And even as I say goodbye, I carry with me the hope that one day, our paths may cross again in a world where love knows no bounds. Until then, I live growing, learning and enjoying life, cherishing the memories we created together, as little as they were, while all the same I gladly listen to our playlist alone.

When silence arrived, it didn’t explain itself. It simply stayed.

And I learned that closure is sometimes something you build alone.

This is where I let go.

No replies expected.
No doors left open.

Just gratitude for what was, and peace in what is.

Zum Mond und zurück, mi dama.
